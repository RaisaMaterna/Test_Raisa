BMC HelixGPT

BMC HelixGPT is an AI-powered digital assistant embedded within the BMC Helix platform. It uses generative AI to help organizations improve IT service management, operations, and also the user experience.

Key Capabilities

  • Natural language interaction for ITSM and ITOM use cases
  • Faster incident resolution through AI-driven insights
  • Automated summaries, human-like interactions, recommendations, and next steps
  • Improved self-service experience for end users

Benefits

By leveraging BMC HelixGPT, organizations can reduce manual effort, accelerate decision-making, and enhance productivity across IT teams.

Who Can Use It?

BMC HelixGPT is designed for IT service desk agents, operations teams, business analysts and users who interact with the BMC Helix platform.
